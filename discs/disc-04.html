Unstable envelopes EP

RDV

Four original tracks from Techno mayhem from Amsterdam based Italian R/D/V and two remixes by Drucal and PTTRNRCRRNT. Since 2015, R/D/V has been steadily building his reputation as a sound engineer and DJ/Producer. The "Unstable Envelopes" EP, his first appearance on Akos Wex's label series Airsound Records. The EP is an excelent showcase of his deep, heavyand synthetic sound. Mastered by Vegim
